The Parsec Definition

A parsec (pc) is the distance at which 1 AU subtends an angle of 1 arcsecond.

d (pc) = 1 / p (arcsec)

This elegant relationship means: larger parallax = closer star.

Proxima Centauri (p = 0.768") is only 1.30 pc away. The Galactic Center (p = 0.000125") is 8000 pc away, at the limit of Gaia's precision.
